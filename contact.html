Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we offer local delivery within a 10-mile radius for orders over $35. Delivery fees start at $5 and vary based on distance.

How far in advance should I order a custom cake?

For standard cakes, we recommend ordering at least 48 hours in advance. For wedding cakes or elaborate designs, please allow 2-3 weeks notice.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we offer gluten-free, vegan, and nut-free options. Please inform us of any allergies or dietary restrictions when placing your order.

Can I place a large catering order?

Absolutely! We cater events of all sizes. Please contact us at least one week in advance for catering orders.
